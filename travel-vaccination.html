Why Travel Vaccines Matter

Traveling to different regions exposes you to diseases not common in your home country. Vaccinations protect both travelers and the populations they visit.

Vaccine Recommendations by Region

  • Sub-Saharan Africa: Yellow fever, Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, Meningitis
  • Southeast Asia: Japanese Encephalitis, Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid
  • South America: Yellow fever, Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Rabies
  • Middle East: Polio, Hepatitis A & B, Meningitis
