1. Introduction

Travel opens the door to new experiences, but it can also expose you to infectious diseases not common in your home country.Whether you're headed to countries with endemic diseases, a remote rainforest, a bustling global city, a mass gathering event or a spiritual pilgrimage, certain vaccinations are essential to protect your health and prevent disease spread across borders.

This guide helps you understand which vaccines you may need, based on your destination and type of travel with the right information about the infectious disease risks, vaccination requirements and links to additional sources of information about travel health protection.

2. Destination-Based Info

Suggested vaccines by region:

  • Africa:Yellow Fever; Hep A, Malaria, Typhoid, Meningococcal Meningitis
  • Southeast Asia: Japanese Encephalitis ,Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid
  • South America: Yellow fever, Hepatitis A, Typhoid; Rabies
  • Middle East:Poliomyelitis, Hepatitis A & B, Meningococcal Meningitis

3. Traveler Categories

  • Pilgrimage: Hajj and Umrah require meningitis and polio vaccinations
  • Humanitarian/Volunteer Work: Hepatitis B, Rabies, Cholera
  • Adventure Travel: Rabies, Typhoid, Hepatitis A
  • Business Travel: Region-specific vaccinations based on length of stay
  • Mass Gatherings Events: Region-specific vaccinations based on length of stay
